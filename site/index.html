Welcome to
HOTEL SAK'CARI EL AMANECER
in San Pedro La Laguna

A Lake front hotel worth visiting!!!

Hotel Sak'cari El Amanecer is an Eco-hotel located on the beautiful shores of Lake Atitlan. It has 20 lovely rooms overlooking the lake, the names sak'cari and amanecer mean sunrise in Tz'tujil and Spanish. The hotel is located in the heart of San Pedro La Laguna within walking distance to fine restaurants, bars and spanish schools. We are now offering 4 beautiful fully furnished apartments that overlook the lake. Each apartment is 2 bedrooms, 1 bath with a large living / dining area and a fully equipped kitchen !!! Whether you're coming for the weekend or for the winter we have got you covered. Come relax with us on the breath taking shores of Lake Atitlan...
